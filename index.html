February 14th
It's the morning of Valentine's Day. You wake up and reach for your phone. You have 1 unread message from Brian.
The Message
It's from Brian. It says:

"Hey. I made you something. Click this link."

There is a link.
Suspicious
Brian replies:

"It's not a virus. I promise. I am a software engineer. I know what a virus is. Please just click it."
Very Suspicious
Brian sends 5 more texts in a row:

"Please."
"It's cute I promise."
"I worked hard on this."
"Laurin."
"Please."

You give in.
Back to Sleep
You go back to sleep. You have a weird dream that someone is building a website about you. Unsettling.

You wake up 30 minutes later. The message is still there. There are now 4 follow-up texts from Brian.
Bold Move
You try to ignore it. Brian has now sent 7 texts and a voice memo. Your phone will not stop buzzing. This is not sustainable.

You open the message.
You're Here
Ok. Welcome to Brian's very important website.

He spent mass time on this so please be nice.
Nice Try
You tried to leave. Unfortunately Brian did not add a back button. He's not great at UI but he is great at trapping you on a webpage.

You're here now.
An Announcement
Brian would like you to know that the last few months have been really great.

Really great. Even that time you watched Beast Games without him. He's over that though (he says).
Getting Somewhere
He also wants you to know that he lets you win at tennis sometimes. Not every time. But sometimes.

He wasn't going to tell you that but the website needed content.
While We're at It
He also gets a little stupid around you sometimes. Like the time he tried to kiss you when you were sick. Not his smoothest moment.

He wasn't going to bring that up but he already typed it so.
The Point of All This
Anyway. Brian has a question.
He's been meaning to ask for a bit now.

Here goes.
Laurin, will you be my Valentine?
 
Happy Valentine's Day, Laurin.
